Information Governance

Establish defensible policies for data retention and disposal before litigation begins. Our governance frameworks reduce risk and lower discovery costs.

In an era of exponential data growth, the biggest risk to an organization is the data they keep but no longer need. Our Information Governance (IG) services help you align your IT infrastructure with legal requirements and business objectives.

Core IG Capabilities

Data Mapping

Comprehensive inventory of data silos, applications, and custodial locations across the enterprise.

Policy Auditing

Review and modernization of retention schedules and legal hold procedures to ensure judicial defensibility.

By implementing a proactive IG strategy, organizations can reduce their "discovery footprint" by identifying and disposing of redundant, obsolete, and trivial (ROT) data before it becomes a liability in litigation.
