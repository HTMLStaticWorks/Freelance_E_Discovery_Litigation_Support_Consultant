The Challenge

The client faced an IP dispute requiring access to email archives from 2008, stored on magnetic tapes and a legacy Exchange server that had not been powered on in a decade.

Our Approach

We deployed a forensic team to the client's off-site storage facility, established a clean-room environment, and successfully imaged the legacy drives using write-blocking technology.
