Cross-Border Data Privacy Compliance

Client

Global Bank

Regions

EU, APAC, NA

Status

GDPR Compliant

The Challenge

A global financial institution required a complex discovery workflow for a regulatory audit involving data across 12 countries. The primary challenge was navigating strict GDPR requirements while ensuring the US-based legal team had access to the necessary evidence.

Our Approach

We implemented a tiered hosting strategy, utilizing regional data centers to ensure data residency compliance. Our automated redaction tools were configured to identify and anonymize PII (Personally Identifiable Information) at the source before any data was transferred across borders.

"LexSupport's deep understanding of international data privacy laws allowed us to meet our discovery obligations without a single compliance breach."
— Head of Compliance
