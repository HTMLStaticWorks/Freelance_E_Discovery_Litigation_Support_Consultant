The Challenge

The client faced a high-stakes patent dispute requiring access to email archives from 2008. The data was stored on a decommissioned Exchange server and magnetic tapes that hadn't been accessed in over a decade.

Our Approach

Our forensic lab established a clean-room environment to revive the hardware. We successfully imaged the drives using hardware write-blockers and utilized legacy extraction tools to reconstruct the database without altering a single bit of metadata.

"The technical credibility LexSupport brought to the stand regarding the recovery process was instrumental in our victory."
— Lead Trial Counsel
